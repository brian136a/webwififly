## üî¥ THE THREE CRITICAL ISSUES

### #1: Data Credibility Crisis (URGENT)
**Page:** [05-Results Analysis](./UX_RESEARCH_05-RESULTS_ANALYSIS.md)  
**Problem:** Test results show 7,000+ Mbps (impossible for home internet)  
**Impact:** User immediately assumes app is broken  
**Fix:** Backend validation, sanity checks, data integrity verification  
**Priority:** FIX THIS FIRST before any user testing

### #2: Privacy Concern Not Addressed (HIGH)
**Page:** [03-Setup Wizard](./UX_RESEARCH_03-SETUP_WIZARD.md)  
**Problem:** App asks for financial data without explaining why or how it's used  
**Impact:** 15-20% of users may abandon due to privacy concern  
**Fix:** Add 2 lines of reassurance copy  
**Priority:** Quick win (5 minutes to implement)

### #3: No Clear Next Steps (HIGH)
**Page:** [05-Results Analysis](./UX_RESEARCH_05-RESULTS_ANALYSIS.md)  
**Problem:** Results shown but no guidance on what they mean or what to do  
**Impact:** User feels informed but not empowered  
**Fix:** Add contextual summary section + CTA  
**Priority:** Needed before monetization

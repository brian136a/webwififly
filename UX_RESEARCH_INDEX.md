## Key Takeaways (1-Minute Read)

‚úÖ **What's Working:**
- Struggle page is exceptional (best part of app)
- Warm, conversational tone throughout
- Setup flow is intuitive and friendly
- Waterfall chart on results is visually clear

‚ùå **What's Broken:**
- Test results show impossible speeds (7,000+ Mbps) ‚Äî URGENT FIX
- Privacy concern not addressed (financial data without explanation)
- No clear guidance after results (user doesn't know what to do)
- Results page is confusing (bars vs. percentages conflict)

üéØ **What to Do:**
1. Fix data integrity (make speeds realistic)
2. Add privacy reassurance in setup
3. Add contextual guidance on results page
4. Introduce next-step CTA (recommendations or premium upsell)
